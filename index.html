Christmas gift exchange

About it

the gift exchange is on December 24th, and you have a budget of $100.

Rules

The rules are you have to buy a gift/gifts that are associated with the color you are given. ie: i have the color red so I can only buy red gifts. Then, on the 24th we will go around in a circle and guess who got them a present, it doesnt matter if you are right or wrong because you will still get your gift.

Other things

you should wear christmas clothing and there will be treats such as cookies and milk, you can bring any other snack you want.
